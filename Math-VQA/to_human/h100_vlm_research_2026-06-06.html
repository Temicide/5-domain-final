Recommendation

Move from Ollama/LLaVA to Qwen/Qwen3-VL-32B-Instruct for first H100 validation, add PaddleOCR-VL OCR/formula text as a second condition, and keep Qwen/Qwen2.5-VL-32B-Instruct as a proven fallback. Start fine-tuning only after a locked validation split; use Qwen3-VL-8B LoRA/QLoRA first because there are only 280 labels.

Why This Direction

Qwen3-VL is the current Qwen VLM family and supports Transformers, vLLM, and SGLang image-text inference. Qwen2.5-VL has published model-card evidence for OCR, DocVQA, MathVista, and MathVision, and supports dynamic-resolution processing. PaddleOCR-VL supports Thai and document elements such as text, formulas, tables, and charts.

Immediate Fix Completed
